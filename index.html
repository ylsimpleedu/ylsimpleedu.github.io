1.1 Science is Part of Daily Life

What is Science?

  1. Science is the knowledge of a discipline that involves systematic observations and investigative experiments on natural phenomena.

  2. The word science comes from the Latin word “scientia” which means knowledge.

  3. Natural phenomena are daily activities or events happening in nature that are not man-made.

1.1 Fields and Careers in Biology

What is Biology?

  1. Biology is the study of living organisms.
  2. The word ‘Biology’ is derived from two Greek words, ‘bios’ which means life and ‘logos’ which means study.
  3. Hence, biology is the study of living things, the environment and the interaction between living things and the environment.

1.1 Physical Quantities

  1. Physical quantities are quantities that can be measured.
    Examples of physical quantities: Length, mass, time, temperature and electric current.

  2. A physical quantity consists of a numerical magnitude with a unit.
    Example: Mass of the Sun = 1 989 000 000 000 000 000 000 000 000 000 kg
    = 1.989 x 10$^{30}$ kg
