P3：《丑小鸭》第一课

Posted on 2026-07-20 Edited on 2026-07-21 Disqus:

第一节课文 · Session 1 Reading Text

IT was so beautiful in the country. It was the summer time. The wheat fields were golden, the oats were green, and the hay stood in great stacks in the green meadows. The stork paraded about among them on his long red legs, chattering away in Egyptian, the language he had learned from his lady mother. All around the meadows and cornfields grew thick woods, and in the midst of the forest was a deep lake. Yes, it was beautiful, it was delightful in the country.

乡村的夏天多么美丽啊！麦田一片金黄，燕麦一片碧绿，青草地上堆着一垛垛干草。一只鹳鸟迈着它那长长的红腿，在草地上踱来踱去，用从妈妈那里学来的埃及话，叽叽喳喳地说个不停。草地和麦田四周都是茂密的树林，树林深处有一个幽静的深湖。是的，乡村美极了，美得让人心里欢喜。

P3：《皇帝的新衣》第一课

Posted on 2026-07-20 Edited on 2026-07-21 Disqus:

第一节课文 · Session 1 Reading Text

Many years ago, there was an Emperor, who was so excessively fond of new clothes, that he spent all his money in dress. He did not trouble himself in the least about his soldiers; nor did he care to go either to the theatre or the chase, except for the opportunities then afforded him for displaying his new clothes. He had a different suit for each hour of the day; and as of any other king or emperor, one is accustomed to say, “he is sitting in council,” it was always said of him, “The Emperor is sitting in his wardrobe.”

从前，有一位皇帝，他非常非常喜欢新衣服，所有的钱都花在穿戴上。他对士兵毫不关心，既不爱去剧院，也不爱去狩猎，除非能够展示他的新衣服。他每个小时都有不同的服装可以换。提到别的国王，人们总说”国王在议政厅”，但提到这位皇帝，人们总说：”皇帝在他的衣帽间里。”

姚老师的共读时光・Reading Together with Teacher Yeow

Posted on 2026-01-22 Edited on 2026-07-20 Disqus:

在短视频和 AI 泛滥的时代，孩子的注意力被算法切割、被多巴胺驱动。AI 可以替孩子输出一切 —— 但无法替孩子思考。

这门课的根本目的，是给孩子每周一次安静读好书的时间和陪伴，帮助他们成为有思考自我意识的真实个体，而不是被动的内容消费者。

In an age of short videos and AI, children’s attention is fragmented by algorithms and driven by dopamine. AI can produce anything on a child’s behalf — but it cannot think for them.

The purpose of this course: to give every child one quiet hour each week to read a good book, with a teacher by their side — and to help them grow into individuals who think for themselves, rather than passive consumers of content.

陪伴・经典・华语・思辨

Companionship · Classics · Chinese · Critical Thinking

课程概览・What We Do

每节课 60 分钟，线上一对一直播（Zoom），全程华语（普通话）授课。

课堂分三个部分：读一段经典文学选段（15–20 分钟）、以 AI 为工具，把当天的阅读与想法转化为可以看见的作品（20–25 分钟）、轻松收尾（5–10 分钟）。课毕，老师发一条极简讯息给家长，说明今天读了什么、孩子有什么反应。

不留课后作业。 这是这门课的核心承诺，不会改变。

Each session runs 60 minutes, conducted one-on-one via Zoom, entirely in Mandarin Chinese.

A class has three parts: reading a selected passage from a classic work of literature (15–20 min), using AI as a tool to turn that session’s reading and ideas into something they can actually see (20–25 min), and a light wrap-up (5–10 min). After each session, the teacher sends a brief note to parents — what was read today, and how the child responded.

No homework. Ever. This is a core promise of the course and will not change.

适合谁・Who Is This For

  • 小学三年级、四年级（P3–P4），8–10 岁
  • 新加坡华裔家庭
  • 希望孩子在屏幕时代保有阅读习惯与独立思考能力的家长
  • Primary 3 and Primary 4 (P3–P4), ages 8–10
  • Chinese families in Singapore
  • Parents who want their child to keep the habit of reading and the ability to think independently in a screen-saturated world
