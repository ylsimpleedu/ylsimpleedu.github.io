1.1 Development in Chemistry Field and Its Importance in Daily Life

Meaning of Chemistry

  1. Chemistry is a field of science that relates to structures, compositions, properties andinteractions between matter.

  2. Knowledge in chemistry is very important for us to understand chemicals and chemical reactions
    around us.

  1. Most activities in daily life involve chemical reactions. For examples, the frying of fish, switching on the torch, cooking and even our body metabolism are chemical reactions.

  2. Pollution in nature involves chemicals such as the occurrence of acid rain and the thinning of the ozone layer.

Chemistry in Daily Life

The chemicals used in daily life are produced by chemical-based industries. The table below shows some examples:

Fields Description
Agriculture Chemical fertilisers are used in agricultural activities to fertilise the soil. Herbicides kill unwanted plants, pesticides kill pests and fungicides kill fungi.
An example of a chemical fertiliser is ammonium sulphate salt.
Food Food such as burger and ice cream contain colouring, preservative, flavouring, antioxidant and stabiliser.
Industry Detergent is produced from processing the by-product of petroleum.
Paint is used to protect the surfaces of materials. It is a type of a polymer.
Other examples of industrial products include glass, ceramic, colouring and alloys.
Medicine Medicine is used to cure diseases.
Examples of medicine are antibiotics, paracetamol and aspirin.
Chemotherapy is a treatment for cancer patients.

The Development in Chemistry Field and the Contributions of Chemical Technology

Industries based on chemicals have contributed greatly to the country’s development in terms of its contribution to the economy, the creation of job opportunities and its advancement in the industrial sector.

Development and contribution
of chemical technology		 Description
Advancement in the industrial sector The products of the chemical industry provide raw materials for other industries. This allows many other industries such as the shipping, manufacturing and heavy machinery industries to expand.
Financial The export of industrially made chemicals such as pesticides is a lucrative source of income to our country.
Job opportunities The chemical industry provides many job opportunities to locals.
